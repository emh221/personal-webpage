About Me

My Software Interests

  • Cloud Infrastructure - I enjoy using Terraform and Cloudformation to provision and create cloud resources. I love the challenge offered by the dynamic nature and broad scope of this work and the detail-oriented approach the work requires.

  • Container Orchestration - The reusability, scalability, and portability of containers excites me as a DevOps engineer. I am very familiar with AWS's ECS offering and am eager to learn more about Kubernetes and the benefits it offers.

  • Automation Tools - The benefits of properly implementing CI/CD are enormous. I am enthusiastic about using tools like Jenkins for build automation to help teams and engineers produce high quality code that can be released to production sooner.
